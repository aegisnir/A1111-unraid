# Contributing

Thanks for taking a look.

This is a personal, AI-assisted hobby project, and I am still learning as I go. Helpful suggestions, corrections, and thoughtful feedback are welcome.

## What is most helpful

The most helpful contributions are usually:
- bug reports with clear reproduction steps
- security-minded feedback
- documentation improvements
- readability improvements
- simpler or safer ways to do the same thing

## Tone and expectations

Please keep feedback constructive and respectful.

I am openly sharing this project while learning, so I expect mistakes and rough edges. I appreciate people pointing those out, especially when the goal is to help improve the project rather than dunk on it.

## Before opening a contribution

If you are planning to suggest a larger change, it may be helpful to open an issue first so we can make sure the direction makes sense.

## Security-related changes

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you are suggesting a security-related improvement, context is especially helpful:
> - what the concern is
> - what risk it may create
> - how your proposed change helps
> - any tradeoffs it introduces

## Scope

In general, I would prefer changes that are:
- simple to understand
- lightweight
- practical for Unraid users
- realistic for a hobbyist to maintain

## No promise of fast review

Because this is a personal project, I cannot promise quick review times, support, or merge timelines.

That said, good-faith feedback is appreciated.
